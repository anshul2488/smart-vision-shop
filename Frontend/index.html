    About this project

    Complete grocery OCR pipeline that parses handwritten grocery lists using advanced AI preprocessing and compares prices from Amazon India, Blinkit, Zepto, and BigBasket.

    Advanced Preprocessing: Line removal, handwriting enhancement, adaptive thresholding, noise removal, deskewing, and multi-version OCR testing.

    Price Comparison: Get real-time prices from Amazon India, Blinkit, Zepto, and BigBasket to find the best deals for your grocery list.
